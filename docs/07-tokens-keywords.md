## Tokens & Keywords

### Keywords

- `let`, `const`, `func`, `class`, `enum`, `global`, `public`, `private`, `async`, `await`, `macro`
- `if`, `else`, `while`, `for`, `in`, `match`, `other`
- `attempt`, `rescue`, `finally`, `assert`, `return`, `break`, `advance`, `leap`
- `new`, `import`, `importpy`, `from`
- `and`, `or`, `not`, `is`

### Literals

- Numbers: `123`, `3.14`
- Char: `'a'` (exactly one character)
- String: `"hello"`, supports escapes (`\n`, `\t`, `\\`, `\"`, `\uXXXX`)
- F-string: `f"Hello {name}"` (expressions inside `{}` are evaluated)
- List: `[1, 2, 3]`
- Dict: `{ "key": 10, "other": 20 }`
- Set: `{ a, b, c}` (no keys)
- Tuple: `(1, 2, 3)` and single-parenthesized value `(x)`
- Booleans: `true`, `false`
- Null: `null`
